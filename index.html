About

I am a working as a Scientist/Engineer-SC at the Space Applications Centre (SAC), Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). Here, my major focus lies in developing advanced image processing and computer vision solutions to enhance radiometric quality of high-resolution satellite imagery. Apart from that, I also indulge myself in designing operational deep learning solutions for image inverse problems and segmentation domain. My research also focuses theoretically analyzying model stability, robustness and convergence properties. I have a deep interest in investigating traditional image processing and optimization techniques to develop reliable cutting edge deep learning solutions.

I received my B.Tech from Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST). I was fornuate to get the opprtunity to work at Virtual Reality (VR) lab under the supervision of Prof. Deepak Mishra during my undergrad research.

Publications

RB-Modulation: Training-Free Personalization of Diffusion Models using Stochastic Optimal Control

NEW Litu Rout, Yujia Chen, Nathaniel Ruiz, Abhishek Kumar, Constantine Caramanis, Sanjay Shakkottai

We introduce Reference-Based Modulation (RB-Modulation), a training-free plug-and-play solution for content and style personalization. By incorporating style features into the controller's terminal cost, we modulate the drift field in diffusion models' reverse dynamics, enabling training-free personalization.

Beyond First-order Tweedie: Solving Inverse Problems using Latent Diffusion

NEW Litu Rout, Yujia Chen, Abhishek Kumar, Constantine Caramanis, Sanjay Shakkottai

We present an efficient second-order approximation using Tweedie’s formula to mitigate the bias incurred in widely used first-order samplers. With this method, we devise a surrogate loss function for every diffusion step to address inverse problems.
